About Me

As a business data analyst, solving business problems, optimizing processes for efficiency and emppowering people to take better busingess decision make me happy. Delivering end-to-end analytics solutions for business problem by working together with different stakeholders/teams is my current area of expertise.

Working as a branch manager for 10 years in one of the leading banks of India, finding any unusual patterns in the financial data of customers has always been a core element of my job. Growing and developing my skills and experience through the years, I always wanted to dive deeper for a more comprehensive understanding of large-scale data and the decisions derived from these data.

I leverage tools such as Excel, SQL, Power BI and Python to uncover hidden trends and patterns, leading to significant efficiency gains and cost reductions. Further, I am eager to apply my skills to transform complex data into predictive analytics through Machine Learning.
